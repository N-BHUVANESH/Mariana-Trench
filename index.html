I won't tell you not to be afraid because the scariest creatures of the deep dwell here, below the Midnight Zone.



15000 feet



The Monsters of out of your worst nightmares pop up.
Anglerfish, for example will scare the heck out of anyone with its long and crooked tooth and a growth on the head that lures the prey.



The Black Swallower, it's an eel-like beast that has a very stretchy stomach and it can swallow prey that's twice it's size.

18900 feet



Look down below and you're gonna see the deepest shipwreck ever found: SS Rio Grande in South Atlantic sunk in 1941 and went as low as 18900 ft.

19700 feet



We are diving into the Mariana Trench



It's both the least explored and the most fascinating area for the scientist and adventurers.

What lies at the bottom of it? Well we are about to see.

26000 feet



Snailfish, it is the deepest fish ever found.It's body is translucent, so you can actually see right through its skin and it withstands the highest pressure.

>26000 feet
